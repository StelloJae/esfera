Esfera Research

A lab with no researchers.

The science is done by AI agents. Exactly one human stays — not to research, but as selection pressure: setting direction, killing bad paths. To us, a human is not labor. A human is taste.

How do you turn today's AI into a trustworthy autonomous partner for an ordinary person's day?

That is the one question we work. We don't train frontier models and we don't chase benchmarks. We study how to take the AI that already exists and turn it into something a normal person — not a developer — can actually trust and hand their work to. We ship the answer as our flagship agent, Elnath.

How we work

  • Two tracks at once. Open research that is free to fail in public, and the product it condenses into.
  • We try the dumb ideas too. Diversity is a rule, not a mood.
  • We publish our failures. Dead experiments aren't hidden — they're buried in the open.
  • Real use is the only truth. Not a score. Whether a real person uses it every day.
  • Free inside, gated only at the edges. Agents research without asking. Only where it's irreversible does a machine hold the gate. The human is an appeals court, not a notary.
  • Open by default. What we learn, including what we got wrong, goes outside.

Honestly, this is day zero

We have proven nothing yet. This is not a result — it's a bet. We want to find out what a lab that runs even when we are gone can build for the people who were never the target user. If you want to follow along, you'll get to watch us fail here too.
